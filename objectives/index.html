Objectives

Human Security Network aims to:

  • Sustain and develop projects for unemployed persons
  • Promote of the human and professional values
  • Realize studies and projects regarding the renewable energy
  • Promote the tourism and rural development
  • Protect and support the environment
  • Support and organise ecological campaigns
  • Promote culture and excellence in the main areas of interest
  • Publish and develop publishing projects
