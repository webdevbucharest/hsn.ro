Projects

Projects history and legal status
Human Security Network Association is a non governmental organisation established in 2008. The aim of the organisation is to develop cultural activities, research, educational projects and consultancy.
The organisation’s objectives target to ensure the proper conditions and the necessary resources for organising and developing activities such as: scientific research, educational projects, public communication, publishing activities, organising cultural and scientific events and activities, conferences, workshops, summer schools, as well as other activities targeting the cultural and scientific fields.

Mission
Human security Network Association promotes, supports and develops projects regarding formal and nonformal education, cultural development, management and leadership, cultural promotion and the highest standards in what concerns culture, science and promotion of the professional values in all the social environments.

Projects and activities
Between 2009-2013, Human Security Network  Association has actively got involved in numerous activities, both as a project coordinator as well as partner.

Projects coordinated by Human Security Network
- Books donations- social and educational project
The project was coordinated by Human Security Network has started in 2009 and it lasted two years, it’s main goal being to increase the degree of cultural and education access. During these two years have been organised several book donation sessions in schools from the rural areas that did not have a library.
- Culture workshop - Started in 2011, the project aim was to organize a series of art and culture workshops for children.  
- e-Culture- Started in 2012, the project is currently ongoing. The main aim of it is to develop modern, efficient and interactive tools to promote the cultural events from Romania, to enable cooperation between museums, cultural centers, libraries and bookstores from Romania and abroad and showcasing Romanian heritage abroad.  

Partnerships
- eSchool - The educational project organised by The Institute for Political Research started in October 2011 and it targeted 17-25 years students from Romania. The project’s purpose was to promote among the young people values such as: liberty, democracy, justice, tradition, respect and  promotion of human rights and to encourage youngsters to express their opinion regarding this values in an essay contest.
